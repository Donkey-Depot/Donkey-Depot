DONKEY DDEPOT

The Challenge Communities in and around Juja face a growing waste management challenge. Valuable organic and recyclable materials are too often discarded, limiting environmental progress and the creation of green economic opportunities. Our Solution Donkey Depot is building a circular economy ecosystem that combines innovative technology with local action. Through the EcoLoop platform and future waste-to-resource initiatives, we aim to transform discarded materials into sustainable products, empower communities, and make responsible resource management simple and rewarding for everyone.
