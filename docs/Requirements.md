## Purpose and Target Audience

The Donkey Depot launch website is intended to introduce the company’s vision and build an engaged community ahead of full operations. The site is designed to serve a broad audience, including individuals of all ages, technology and sustainability enthusiasts, local businesses, prospective partners, and potential investors.

## Project Scope

The website will include the following sections:

* A Home page presenting the company’s mission, roadmap, and newsletter signup.
* An About page describing the company’s vision, philosophy, and leadership team.
* A Products & Solutions page showcasing upcoming products and partnership opportunities.
* An EcoLoop Preview page featuring the next generation of the EcoLoop application and a beta waitlist.
* A Contact page allowing visitors to connect with the team and become involved in the community.

## Functional Requirements

Visitors shall be able to:

* Browse and access all website content on any supported device.
* Subscribe to the newsletter and join the EcoLoop beta waitlist.
* Submit inquiries and partnership requests through contact forms.
* Learn about the company’s mission, products, and future plans.

## Non-Functional Requirements

The website shall provide a fast, secure, and intuitive user experience. It must be fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, and its codebase should be modular to facilitate future integration with the EcoLoop application.

## Timeline and Acceptance Criteria

The project shall be completed in accordance with the schedule defined in the Statement of Work. The website will be considered complete upon successful implementation of all items in the approved task list and satisfaction of the agreed quality standards.
