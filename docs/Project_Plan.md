Project Plan
1. Project Overview

This project covers the design, development, testing, and delivery of the Donkey Depot launch website and the EcoLoop rebuild preview environment in accordance with the approved requirements and Statement of Work.

2. Project Phases
Phase 1: Discovery & Design (08 July 2026 – 01 August 2026)
Review requirements and finalize information architecture.
Create wireframes and visual mockups.
Obtain stakeholder approval.
Phase 2: Core Development (02 August 2026 – 01 September 2026)
Implement the website structure and reusable components.
Develop the newsletter, contact, and beta waitlist backends.
Establish the staging environment.
Phase 3: Content Integration & Feature Completion (02 September 2026 – 01 October 2026)
Integrate all approved content and media assets.
Complete animations and responsive layouts.
Perform an initial quality assurance review.
Phase 4: Testing & Optimization (02 October 2026 – 01 November 2026)
Conduct cross-browser and cross-device testing.
Optimize performance, accessibility, and security.
Resolve identified defects.
Phase 5: Final Delivery (02 November 2026 – 18 November 2026)
Prepare technical documentation.
Deliver the final source code and assets.
Complete final review and acceptance.
3. Tools and Communication

Project progress will be tracked through the company’s designated project management and version-control platforms, with regular status updates provided throughout the project.

4. Risk Management

Potential delays caused by late content, feedback, or technical changes will be communicated promptly, and the schedule will be adjusted in accordance with the agreed change-management process.

5. Completion Criteria

The project will be considered complete upon successful delivery and acceptance of all agreed deliverables and tasks in accordance with the approved requirements and quality standards.
